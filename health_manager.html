Health Manager

Helping students to eat on a healthy and cheap diet

Solution Overview

To better protect students' bodies, I came up with a mobile app with a search board, a check list and a freezer page. Functions of this app are exploring the wholesome food, offering markets' locations, comparing prices, recommendations for a healthy diet and reminders for the expiration date of the food.This app could take care of the students' eating habits and solve their issues from buying food to consuming food.

Research

To better understand my design challenge and the actual problems the students are facing in the universities, I investigated target users and find out what are their demands when living far away from parents.

Understanding Users

First, after the investigation, I have learned about the demand of users. I divided them into two types, cook and don't cook. They have commons and differences. Both types want to change their eating habit, buy food at lower price and eat diversily.

Secondly, I have learned about critical issues and eating habits from users. They concerned about the distance of the market and care more about diversity of the food sold in the market.

Thirdly, according to previous investigation and analysis, I made three personas representing three main types of students.

Marketing Research

Based on the Interviews, I collected the important features. Based on these features, I chose four apps as competitors. It seems like that some necessary functions are not combined and the scope of the apps are not highly related with the demand of the college students.

Six Solutions

Based on the research, I started the design through sketches, ranging from mobile app to VR markets. I have came up with six solutions to help the students solve four problems: where to buy, what is healthier, what is cheaper, when should eat.

Work Flow

Depending on the users' demands, the whole working process was clarified to make sure the flow is workable and smooth.

Paper Prototype

I sketched draft of mobile interfaces on the paper, which contains the functions of exploring, managing the freezer, checklist and profile. This helps to check the whole workflow.
